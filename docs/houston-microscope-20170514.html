Premise:

The decline of a galactic patrol divides a hostile ecosystem.

  • Period, Start: Home-system of conquering species, "Supremes," is destroyed by a supernova.
    • Event: Admiral Torkid (a Supreme) establishes martial law on the planet of Maris.
    • Event: The Supremes decrease their efforts to hunt pirates.
    • Event: The Textractiic Military Monastic Order begins hostile broadcasts originating from Home-System Cluster 6.
  • Period: Subjugated planets & societies struggle to cope with the power vaccum left by the loss of the Supreme home-system.
    • Event: The Kontrid Matriarch, Kastus, is found dead in her bed by her servants in the morning.
      • Scene: Was there an assassination attempt? Yes, by Ryan alone. This was determined in a scene occurring the night before the murder, in which Kastus' daughter, Karmen; a Supreme soldier, Ryan; and the Manor Captain of the Guard, Rankus, are all present. Kastus' aunt, Tanar, is notably not present. Rankus has summoned both Karmen and Ryan to inform them of intrusion attempts over the past two weeks. Ryan implies that Karmen may have motive to kill her mother in the future, causing her to storm off in response. After Karmen's departure, Ryan and Rankus have a hostile interaction. Ryan attempts to talk Rankus into killing Kastus, but is unsuccessful.
    • Event: Karmen duels Tonar over the right to succession, and ascends to the position of Matriarch by slaying her in single combat.
    • Event: All of the data and records used by Kastus to reign as Matriarch have been destroyed, stolen, or otherwise compromised. Rankus is missing, and there are signs of a struggle.
    • Event: Karmen returns to the Matriarchal Manor after slaying Tonar and discovers the total disarried state of the manor after the data compromise.
  • Period: Karmen reigns as the Kontrid Matriarch.
    • Event: Karmen begins a full-scale personnel purge of the government as previously appointed by Kastus.
    • Event: A convent of Textractic nuns saves Karmen's younger sister from the purge.
  • Period: Planetary ecosystems all over the galaxy begin to experience rapid & brutal decay.
    • Event: The tomb of Matriarch Kastus is pillaged by pirates.
    • Event: A data chip found in the body of Matriarch Kastus contains information about experimental technology related to the supernova that destroyed the Supreme home-system.
      • Scene: While the captain of the pirate ship is not present, the on-board scientist (a female Kontrid captive of the pirates) and the First Mate discuss whether or not they should tell the captain about the data chip. The Scientist mentions that the encryption on the data chip is beyond her abilities to decrypt, but that they might be able to take the information to be decrypted by the Textractic Order. They decide to conceal the information, but At-At, a ship's hand, knows about the conversation.
    • Event: At-At betrays the Scientist & the First Mate by telling the ship's captain about the data chip. However, the Scientisc and the First Mate have successfully escaped with the data chip to a convent of the Textractic Order. They discover that the residents of the Textractic convent are the only suvivors on the Kontrid planet.
    • Event: A supervolvano erupts on Maris.
    • Event: Refugees from Maris enter the nearby Home-System Cluster 6. Everyone is dead, and the planets are devoid of life.
    • Event: The nuns of the Textractic Order decode the data chip found in the body of Matriarch Kastus. It reveals that the Supremes discovered a method of manipulating matter and energy. They attempted to use it on their Sun, but it resulted in apocalyptic disaster.
  • Period, End: Mass extragalactic migration begins.
    • Event: The first successful extragalactic colony is modelled on the governance of Karmen, who left a lasting legacy after her time as the Kontrid Matriarch.
Legacies in play at end of session:
  • A.E.: Pirates
  • S.M.: Data Chip
  • G.J.: Matriarch Karmen vs Total Data Loss
