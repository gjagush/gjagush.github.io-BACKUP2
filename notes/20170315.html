      • Travel Gavel
        • Fort Worth Lodge #148 had its stated meeting.
        • A bunch of guys from Handley Lodge showed up.
        • Some guys from Webb Lodge did, too.
        • They came to take the travelling gavel!
        • We had brisket cooked on the Fort Worth Lodge smoker.
        • We ran out of aprons and had to go for backups - we had a total of 54 brothers at the meeting.
        • All the new visitors got FWL148 pins.
        • We added the Fort Worth Lodge Mega Gavel to the game, in addition to the plaque, which we presented to their current Worshipful Master, Haskell Rogers III.
        • Check out more details and pictures at texanmason.com or the Fort Worth Lodge #148 FB page.

    • National
      • Warren Lodge #310 under the Grand Lodge of Pennsylvania, F&AM recently conferred a special Master Mason's degree. The candidate of the degree is Pennsylvania's only member who uses sign language as his primary means of communication, so when it was time to conferr his Master's Degree, they were interpreted and conferred in American Sign Language.
      • The Grand Lodge of Mississippi, F&AM, has removed their recognition of the Grand Lodge of Bulgaria. Haven't been able to find a lot of info other than the GL of Mississippi claimed this was because of unmasonic activities. Haven't been able to confirm anything on this.
