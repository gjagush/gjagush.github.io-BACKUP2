      • Northern Masonic Jurisdiction of the Scottish Rite - the SRNMJ Supreme Council is releasing a comprehensive guidebook for their degrees! This is something that SR Masons in the NMJ have really been wanting for a long time. The NMJ ritual work is extremely different from SR ritual elsewhere in the world, and there's not a huge amount of resources on it. This is a big step as the last pretty good guidebook for the NMJ was published a hundred years ago. The book is available for $27 and shipping from Cornerstone Publishing.
